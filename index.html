About The College

Jyothy Institute of Technology


Jyothy Institute of Technology was established in the year 2011 under the leadership of Karnataka Rajyothsava Awardee, Dharmika Pravara, Late Dr. B.N.V Subrahmanya, visionary par excellence. Since inception JIT has grown to be one of the reputed technical institutions in the city of Bengaluru. The main focus of our Institution is to empower the students with the kind of skill sets desired by the industry. This would help all students to compete in the highly demanding global industrial market. Our institution is blessed with a pool of highly qualified, talented, committed teaching and support staff who ensure a worthy learning environment for our students
